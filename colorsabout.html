ABOUT:

proj_hu_vr is a Three.js project consisting of 3 kaleidoscopic virtual terrains (above) built from a collection of abstract watercolor paintings.

(WARNING: to avoid slow first time environment loading times, you can watch demos of these landscapes here _mars , _glitch , _hyperbolic )

For camera control, if using a mouse, left click drag is pan motion, scroll is zoom forward or backward, and right click drag is rotate. If using a touch control, one touch is pan motion and two touches is zoom and rotation.

This was my first project in Three.js and I wanted to make sure I used this opportunity to create some of my wildest dreams in terms of interactive art.
